Interactive 3D Model of a Panamax Ship

This interactive 3D model allows you to explore the composition of a panamax ship and learn about its capabilities. Please see the direction below in order to interact with the 3D model.


360 Tumble or Spin

Click and drag using the left mouse button

pan_tool_alt Pan or Slide

Click and drag using the right mouse button

zoom_out_map Zooming in or out

Manipulate the middle mouse button or scroll wheel

Remember to play the audio narration as your interact with the 3D model
